# My PMP
a clip-on sport media player built for running.

no phone, no earphones, no fuss. clips onto your waistband, plays music through a speaker, and gets out of the way.

## the idea
wanted something that just plays music while running without dealing with a phone bouncing in a pocket or wires getting in the way. no buttons either. shake to wake, touch to interact, clip on and forget it.

## how it works
- shake the device to wake the screen
- browse and pick songs via touch
- autoplays to next track, no interaction needed while running
- speaker on the back, behind a grille
- spring clip on the back attaches to a waistband

## transferring songs
esp32 hosts a wifi access point. connect your phone or laptop to it, open a browser, drag and drop songs. no app, no cable, no card reader needed.
